Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I register for an event?

Simply browse our events, click on the one you're interested in, and follow the registration process. It's quick, free, and secure!

Is TapNex free to use?

Yes! TapNex is completely free for attendees. Event organizers also have access to our free tier with generous limits.

How do I become an event organizer?

Contact us through this form or email info@tapnex.tech to get started as an organizer. We'll guide you through the setup process.

Can I cancel my event registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration anytime before the event. Check your confirmation email for cancellation instructions or contact the organizer.
