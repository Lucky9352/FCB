Shipping & Delivery Policy

Service Delivery Information

Important Notice

TapNex (NEXGEN FC) is an on-premise gaming cafe service. We do not ship or deliver physical products. All services are provided at our physical location.

Nature of Service

Our business model is based on location-based gaming services. Customers book gaming sessions online and visit our cafe to enjoy:

  • High-performance gaming PCs and consoles
  • Popular multiplayer and single-player games
  • Comfortable gaming environment
  • Refreshments and snacks (where available)
  • Professional gaming equipment and accessories

Cancellation & Modifications

Since we don't ship products, our cancellation policy is based on booking modifications:

  • Free Cancellation: Up to 24 hours before scheduled session
  • Late Cancellation: 50% refund if cancelled within 24 hours
  • No-Show: No refund for missed bookings without cancellation
  • Rescheduling: Free rescheduling available (subject to availability)

For detailed refund information, please refer to our Refund Policy.
