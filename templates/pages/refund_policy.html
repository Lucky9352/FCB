Cancellation and Refund Policy

This policy outlines the terms and conditions for cancellations and refunds on forge.tapnex.tech.

!

Strict No-Refund Policy

All bookings made through forge.tapnex.tech are final and non-refundable. Once payment is confirmed and the booking is processed, no refunds will be provided under any circumstances.

No Exceptions for Standard Cases

This includes but is not limited to: no-shows, change of plans, dissatisfaction with venue experience, personal emergencies, or any other circumstances.

Why This Policy?

Gaming slot reservations are time-sensitive and exclusive. When you book a slot, it is reserved exclusively for you during that specific time period. This ensures fair access for all users and prevents last-minute cancellations that could affect other customers.

  • Slots are reserved exclusively for confirmed bookings
  • Prevents unfair advantage over other customers waiting for slots
  • Ensures reliable scheduling for Forge Gaming operations

No Refunds Will Be Given For

The following situations do not qualify for refunds:

Failure to arrive for your booked slot
Change of plans or personal reasons
Dissatisfaction with gaming equipment
Venue ambiance or atmosphere
Technical issues with games
Staff service quality
Weather conditions
Any other unforeseen circumstances

Exception Clause

Refunds may only be processed if explicitly requested in writing by Forge Gaming through their official email or communication channels. This is extremely rare and only applies in exceptional circumstances determined solely by Forge Gaming management.

Processing Fees

In the unlikely event that a refund is approved, processing fees and platform charges may apply.

Contact for Payment Errors

If you experience a genuine payment processing error (such as duplicate charges or incorrect amounts), please contact our support team immediately:

support@tapnex.tech

Please include your booking reference number and payment details. Payment error claims will be investigated and resolved within 7-10 business days.

Please Read Carefully

By making a booking on forge.tapnex.tech, you acknowledge and agree to this strict no-refund policy. Please ensure you are certain about your booking before completing payment.

⚠️ Bookings are final. No refunds will be provided.
