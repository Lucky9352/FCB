Refund & Cancellation Policy

Our cancellation and refund terms

Last Updated: November 1, 2025

Important: Strict No-Refund Policy

Due to the nature of our gaming slot booking service, all bookings are final and non-refundable. Please read this policy carefully before making a booking.

1. Policy Overview

TapNex Gaming Cafe operates a strict no-refund policy for all gaming slot bookings made through our platform at forge.tapnex.tech.

Once a booking is confirmed and payment is processed, it cannot be cancelled, modified, or refunded under any circumstances.

2. Reason for No-Refund Policy

This policy exists because:

  • Gaming slots are time-specific and cannot be resold once booked
  • We allocate resources and staff based on confirmed bookings
  • Other customers may have been turned away for your time slot
  • Gaming equipment and stations are reserved exclusively for your booking

3. No Cancellation

Bookings cannot be cancelled for any reason, including but not limited to:

  • Change of plans or schedule conflicts
  • Personal emergencies
  • Weather conditions
  • Technical issues on your end
  • Booking the wrong date or time
  • Finding a better price elsewhere

4. No Refunds

Refunds will not be issued under any circumstances, including:

  • No-shows or late arrivals
  • Duplicate bookings made in error
  • Dissatisfaction with the service
  • Technical difficulties preventing you from attending
  • Cancellation by the customer for any reason

5. No Rescheduling

Bookings cannot be rescheduled to a different date or time. If you cannot attend your booked slot, you will forfeit your booking and payment without any compensation or credit.

6. No Transfers

Bookings are non-transferable and cannot be given, sold, or transferred to another person without prior written approval from TapNex Gaming Cafe.

7. Limited Exceptions

Refunds or cancellations may only be considered in the following rare circumstances:

  • Technical Failure by TapNex: If our systems fail and we cannot provide the booked service
  • Cafe Closure: If the gaming cafe is forced to close due to unforeseen circumstances beyond our control
  • Double Booking Error: If we accidentally double-book a slot due to a system error

Even in these cases, refunds are issued at TapNex's sole discretion and must be requested in writing within 24 hours of the incident.

8. Payment Issues

If you experience payment issues:

  • Duplicate Charges: If your card is charged multiple times for the same booking due to a technical error, the duplicate charge will be refunded within 7-10 business days
  • Payment Failure: If payment fails but money is deducted, it will be refunded by your bank/payment processor within 5-7 business days

9. Chargeback Warning

Warning: Initiating a chargeback with your bank/credit card company for a valid booking is considered fraud. This may result in:

  • Permanent ban from TapNex Gaming Cafe
  • Legal action to recover costs
  • Blacklisting from future bookings

10. Before You Book

Please ensure before booking:

  • You have selected the correct date and time
  • You have chosen the right game and booking type
  • You are available to attend at the scheduled time
  • You understand and accept this no-refund policy
  • You have read all booking terms and conditions

11. Acknowledgment

By completing a booking on TapNex Gaming Cafe, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Refund and Cancellation Policy. You confirm that you accept the no-refund, no-cancellation, and no-rescheduling terms.
