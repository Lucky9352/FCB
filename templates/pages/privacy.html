Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data from users of forge.tapnex.tech:

  • Name, Email, Phone: Collected from the User model during account registration
  • Booking Details: Information about gaming slot reservations and bookings

How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used for the following purposes:

  • Process and manage gaming slot bookings
  • Send booking confirmations and digital passes via email
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect platform security
4

Data Sharing

We share your data only when necessary and in accordance with applicable laws:

  • With Forge Gaming: Booking details are shared to facilitate your gaming session
  • With Razorpay: Payment information for processing transactions
  • With Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect platform security

Important: We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure and regular security updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update your information via your profile settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
