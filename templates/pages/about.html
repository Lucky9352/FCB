About TapNex Gaming Cafe

Revolutionizing gaming cafe experiences

Who We Are

Welcome to TapNex Gaming Cafe, India's premier gaming slot booking platform. We're transforming how gamers connect with gaming cafes across the country.

Built on cutting-edge technology and powered by passion for gaming, TapNex provides a seamless, hassle-free booking experience for gamers and cafe owners alike.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the gaming cafe industry by providing an intuitive, reliable, and efficient booking platform that empowers gamers and cafe owners.

Our Vision

To become the go-to platform for gaming cafe bookings across India, creating a thriving ecosystem for the gaming community.

What We Offer

For Gamers

  • Instant slot booking with real-time availability
  • Secure payment processing via Razorpay
  • Easy Google OAuth login - no password hassles
  • Personalized dashboard to track bookings
  • Browse and select from multiple games

For Cafe Owners

  • Comprehensive cafe management dashboard
  • Automated slot generation and scheduling
  • Revenue tracking and financial reports
  • Real-time booking notifications
  • Full control over pricing and availability
